Disease Information

Breast Cancer

Symptoms: Lump in breast, change in size, pain.

Causes: Genetic mutations, age, lifestyle.

Treatment: Surgery, chemotherapy, radiation.

Prevention: Regular screening, healthy lifestyle.

Diabetes

Symptoms: Increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue.

Causes: Insulin resistance, genetics, obesity.

Treatment: Insulin, lifestyle changes, medication.

Prevention: Healthy diet, exercise, weight management.

Covid-19

Symptoms: Fever, cough, shortness of breath, fatigue.

Causes: Coronavirus (SARS-CoV-2) infection.

Treatment: Rest, hydration, antiviral drugs (severe cases).

Prevention: Vaccination, mask, hand hygiene.

Cancer

Symptoms: Varies by type; weight loss, fatigue, lumps.

Causes: Genetic mutations, environmental factors.

Treatment: Surgery, chemotherapy, radiation, immunotherapy.

Prevention: Healthy lifestyle, regular screening.

Hypertension

Symptoms: Often none; sometimes headache, dizziness.

Causes: Obesity, genetics, high salt intake.

Treatment: Lifestyle changes, blood pressure medications.

Prevention: Healthy diet, exercise, low sodium.

Asthma

Symptoms: Wheezing, coughing, shortness of breath.

Causes: Allergens, pollution, genetics.

Treatment: Inhalers, steroids, avoid triggers.

Prevention: Allergen control, medications.

Malaria

Symptoms: Fever, chills, vomiting, headache.

Causes: Plasmodium parasites via mosquito bites.

Treatment: Antimalarial drugs.

Prevention: Mosquito nets, repellents, prophylactic meds.

Dengue

Symptoms: High fever, rash, joint pain, bleeding.

Causes: Dengue virus via Aedes mosquito.

Treatment: Fluids, pain relievers (no aspirin), hospital care.

Prevention: Avoid mosquito bites, eliminate breeding areas.

Typhoid

Symptoms: Fever, weakness, stomach pain, rash.

Causes: Bacteria (Salmonella typhi) via contaminated food/water.

Treatment: Antibiotics, fluids, rest.

Prevention: Safe drinking water, hygiene, vaccination.

Cholera

Symptoms: Severe diarrhea, dehydration, weakness.

Causes: Vibrio cholerae bacteria from dirty water.

Treatment: ORS, IV fluids, antibiotics (severe cases).

Prevention: Clean water, sanitation, hand washing.
